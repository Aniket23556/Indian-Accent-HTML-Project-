Our Story

Indian Accent showcases inventive Indian cuisine by complementing the flavours and traditions of India with global ingredients and techniques. Indian Accent, New Delhi has won numerous awards and honours from diners and critics across the world. It was voted the No. 1 Restaurant in India by Conde Nast Traveller and has been on Asia's 50 Best Restaurants list from 2015, currently ranked #19. It has also been recognized by Time Magazine amongst the 'World's 100 Greatest Places'.
