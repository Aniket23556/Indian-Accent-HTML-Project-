Introduction

"Indian Accent" is a renowned restaurant located in New Delhi, India. It is known for its innovative approach to Indian cuisine, offering a modern and creative twist to traditional dishes. The restaurant has gained international acclaim for its unique culinary creations, presentation, and a sophisticated dining experience. The chef, Manish Mehrotra, is celebrated for his inventive take on Indian flavors.

Indian Aceent, New Delhi has won numerous awards and honours from diners and critics across the world. It was voted the No. 1 Restaurant in India by Conde Nast Traveller and has been on Asia's 50 Best Restaurants list from 2015, currently ranked #19. It has also been recognized by Time Magazine amongst the 'World's 100 Greatest Places'
